Hotel System Guide

Everything you need to get started quickly

This guide covers all features available in the Hotel Reservation System. Sections marked ADMIN are only accessible to administrators.

Receptionist
  • View and manage guest profiles
  • Create, confirm & cancel reservations
  • Check guests in and out
  • Check room availability
  • Mark rooms as Available after checkout
  • View bills (read-only)
Administrator
  • Everything a receptionist can do
  • Process payments & apply discounts
  • Add charges / adjustments to bills
  • Add, edit and delete rooms
  • Manage staff accounts
  • View occupancy, revenue & activity reports

How to Log In
  1. 1Open the system in your browser. You will see the Login page.
  2. 2Enter your Username (provided by your admin) and Password.
  3. 3Click Sign In. You will be taken to the Dashboard.
  4. 4Your role (Admin / Receptionist) is shown in the top-right corner.
Tip: If you see "Invalid credentials", check caps lock or ask your admin to reset your password.
Navigating the System

Use the left sidebar to navigate between pages:

  • Dashboard — hotel overview & stats
  • Reservations — book, check-in, check-out
  • Guests — guest profiles
  • Rooms — availability & status
  • Billing — bills & payments
  • Reports ADMIN — analytics
  • Staff ADMIN — staff accounts
To log out, scroll to the bottom of the sidebar and click Logout.

Create a Reservation
  1. 1Go to Reservations page.
  2. 2Click New Reservation.
  3. 3Select the Guest (type to search, or add a new guest first).
  4. 4Choose Room Type, then pick an available room from the list.
  5. 5Set Check-in and Check-out dates.
  6. 6Click Confirm Reservation.
Check In a Guest
  1. 1Find the reservation (search by name or reservation number).
  2. 2Reservation must be in CONFIRMED status.
  3. 3Click the reservation row to open details.
  4. 4Click Check In. Status changes to CHECKED_IN.
After check-in, the room status automatically becomes OCCUPIED.
Check Out a Guest
  1. 1Go to Billing and find the guest's bill.
  2. 2Review the total amount (nightly rate × nights).
  3. 3An admin must process the payment.
  4. 4Once paid, go to Reservations and click Check Out.
  5. 5The room is automatically marked AVAILABLE.
Reservation Statuses
CONFIRMED
Reservation created, guest hasn't arrived yet.
CHECKED_IN
Guest is currently staying in the room.
CHECKED_OUT
Guest has left and bill is settled.
CANCELLED
Reservation was cancelled before arrival.

Adding a New Guest
  1. 1Go to Guests page.
  2. 2Click Add Guest.
  3. 3Fill in Full Name, Phone, and optionally Email and ID Number.
  4. 4Click Save. The guest is now available when creating reservations.
Always add the guest profile before creating their reservation.
Searching & Editing Guests
  • Use the search bar to find a guest by name, phone, or email.
  • Click the pencil icon next to a guest to edit their details.
  • Click the trash icon to delete a guest. Note: guests with active reservations cannot be deleted.

Check Availability
  1. 1Go to Rooms page.
  2. 2In the Check Room Availability section, select a Room Type (optional).
  3. 3Enter Check-in and Check-out dates.
  4. 4Click Check Availability. Available rooms will be listed grouped by type.
Mark a Room as Available
  1. 1In the rooms list, find a room that shows OCCUPIED or MAINTENANCE.
  2. 2Click the green Mark Available button next to it.
  3. 3Confirm the action. The room is now AVAILABLE for new reservations.
Rooms are also auto-released when payment is fully processed.
Manage Rooms ADMIN
  1. 1Click Add Room (top-right of the rooms list).
  2. 2Fill in Room Number, Floor, Type, and Status.
  3. 3To edit a room, click the pencil icon.
  4. 4To delete a room, click the trash icon. Rooms with active reservations cannot be deleted.
Room Types
Single
One guest, standard amenities
Double
Two guests, spacious room
Deluxe
Premium furnishings, extra space
Suite
Luxury suite, full amenities

Generate a Bill
  1. 1Go to Billing page.
  2. 2Click Generate Bill.
  3. 3Search for the reservation by number or guest name and select it.
  4. 4The bill is calculated automatically: nightly rate × number of nights.
  5. 5Click Generate. The bill appears in the list as PENDING.
Process Payment ADMIN
  1. 1Find the bill in the list and click View / Pay.
  2. 2Admins can add Discounts or extra Charges before payment.
  3. 3Click Process Payment. Enter admin credentials if prompted.
  4. 4Choose payment method and confirm the amount.
  5. 5Bill status changes to PAID. The room is automatically released.
Only administrators can process payments, apply discounts, or add charges. Receptionists can view bills but not modify them.
Bill Statuses
PENDING
Bill generated, payment not yet received.
PARTIAL
Partial payment received; balance remaining.
PAID
Full payment received. Room released.

Generating Reports
  1. 1Go to Reports (admin only).
  2. 2Select a period: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Yearly, or Custom date range.
  3. 3Click Generate Report. All five report types will load below.
Report Types
  • Occupancy — room utilisation rates per type
  • Revenue — total income by day or period
  • Guest History — guest activity and visit counts
  • Staff Activity — reservations handled per staff
  • Status Summary — current reservation status breakdown

Adding a New Staff Member
  1. 1Go to Staff page (admin only).
  2. 2Click Add Staff.
  3. 3Enter Full Name, Username, and Password.
  4. 4Select their Role: Admin or Receptionist.
  5. 5Email and Phone are optional but recommended.
  6. 6Click Save. The staff member can now log in.
Editing & Removing Staff
  • Click the pencil icon to edit a staff member's name, role, or password.
  • Click the trash icon to remove a staff member.
Staff with existing reservations linked to their account cannot be deleted. Reassign those reservations first.
You cannot delete your own account while logged in.
